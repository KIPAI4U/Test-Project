Our Remodeling Process

A proven approach that keeps your project on time, on budget, and stress-free from start to finish.

1

Consultation

Every great remodel starts with a conversation.

We begin with a free in-home consultation where we sit down with you to understand your goals, discuss your vision, and evaluate the scope of the project. We'll talk about your lifestyle, what's working and what isn't in your current space, your design preferences, and your budget range.

This meeting helps us understand exactly what you need so we can provide thoughtful recommendations and a realistic roadmap for your project.

What to Expect

  • 60-90 minute in-home visit
  • Discussion of goals, scope, and budget
  • Initial design ideas and feasibility assessment
  • No obligation, no pressure
2

Design & Planning

Turning your vision into a detailed plan.

Our design team creates detailed layouts and material selections tailored to your goals. We help you choose finishes, fixtures, and materials that fit your style and budget. You'll see your project take shape through floor plans and detailed specifications.

We guide you through every decision so nothing is left to chance. By the end of this phase, you'll know exactly what your remodel will look like.

What to Expect

  • Detailed floor plans and layouts
  • Material and finish selections
  • Fixture and appliance guidance
  • Design revisions until you're satisfied
3

Project Proposal

Clear pricing and a realistic timeline.

We provide a detailed written proposal that includes every element of your project. You'll see a line-by-line breakdown of costs, a clear construction timeline, and a scope of work that covers all materials, labor, and project management.

There are no hidden fees or surprise charges. We believe in full transparency so you can make confident decisions about your investment.

What to Expect

  • Detailed written estimate
  • Line-by-line cost breakdown
  • Project timeline with milestones
  • Complete scope of work document
4

Construction

Professional craftsmanship with consistent communication.

Our experienced crew brings your design to life with precision and care. We protect your home during construction, maintain a clean jobsite, and keep you informed every step of the way with regular updates on progress and any decisions that arise.

Your dedicated project manager is your single point of contact throughout construction, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.

What to Expect

  • Dedicated project manager
  • Daily jobsite cleanup
  • Regular progress updates
  • Quality inspections at every phase
5

Final Walkthrough

Ensuring everything exceeds your expectations.

Before we hand over the keys to your new space, we conduct a thorough final walkthrough together. We inspect every detail, test every fixture, and make sure everything meets our standards and yours.

If anything needs adjustment, we address it before the project is complete. Your satisfaction is the only measure of success.

What to Expect

  • Detailed walk-through of all work
  • Punch list review and completion
  • Care and maintenance guidance
  • Warranty documentation
