Our Remodeling Process

A clear, proven approach to transforming your home — from first call to final walkthrough.

01

Consultation

We visit your home, listen to your goals, and assess the project scope. This is a no-pressure conversation where we get to know your vision, lifestyle, and budget expectations.

What to Expect

  • In-home visit and space assessment
  • Discussion of your goals and priorities
  • Budget range conversation
  • Timeline overview
  • Q&A with your project consultant
02

Design & Planning

Our design team creates detailed plans tailored to your vision. We select materials, finalize layouts, and refine every detail until the design is exactly right.

What to Expect

  • Detailed design concepts and floor plans
  • Material and finish selections
  • 3D renderings (for qualifying projects)
  • Design revision rounds
  • Permit coordination
03

Project Proposal

You receive a clear, transparent proposal with fixed pricing, detailed scope of work, and a realistic timeline. No surprises, no hidden fees.

What to Expect

  • Itemized cost breakdown
  • Fixed-price contract
  • Detailed project timeline
  • Material specifications
  • Warranty information
04

Construction

Our skilled crew brings your design to life with quality craftsmanship. You'll receive daily progress updates and have a dedicated project manager as your single point of contact.

What to Expect

  • Dedicated project manager
  • Daily progress updates
  • Clean and organized job site
  • Regular quality inspections
  • On-schedule milestone tracking
05

Final Walkthrough

We walk through every detail together to make sure you love the finished result. We don't consider a project complete until you're completely satisfied.

What to Expect

  • Detailed quality walkthrough
  • Punch list completion
  • Appliance and system orientation
  • Warranty documentation
  • Maintenance guide for your new space

The first step is a free consultation. Let's talk about your project.
