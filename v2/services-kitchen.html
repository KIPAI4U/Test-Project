Kitchen Remodeling

Custom kitchens designed for how you live.

Service Overview

Your kitchen is the heart of your home, and it should reflect how you actually live. At GRIT Builders, we design and build custom kitchens that combine beautiful aesthetics with everyday functionality. From layout optimization to finish selection, we handle every detail so you can enjoy a kitchen that works as hard as you do.

What's Included

  • Custom cabinetry
  • Countertop fabrication & installation
  • Kitchen island design
  • Backsplash tile
  • Plumbing fixtures & sinks
  • Lighting design
  • Appliance installation
  • Flooring
  • Electrical upgrades
  • Painting & trim

Popular Features

Open Layouts

Remove walls and open up your kitchen to create a bright, connected living space perfect for entertaining.

Custom Islands

Purpose-built islands with integrated storage, seating, and prep space tailored to your workflow.

Premium Countertops

Quartz, granite, and natural stone surfaces fabricated and installed with precision.

Smart Storage

Pull-out shelves, drawer organizers, and pantry systems that maximize every inch of space.
