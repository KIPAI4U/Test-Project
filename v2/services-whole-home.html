Whole-Home Remodeling

Service Overview

A whole-home remodel is the most comprehensive way to transform your living space. GRIT Builders coordinates every trade, material, and timeline so your entire home is renovated efficiently and beautifully. From kitchens and bathrooms to flooring, lighting, and trim, we manage the full scope so you have one team, one point of contact, and one cohesive result.

What's Included

  • Design consultation
  • Space planning
  • Kitchen remodel
  • Bathroom remodel
  • Flooring throughout
  • Electrical upgrades
  • Plumbing updates
  • Painting
  • Trim & millwork
  • Project management
