Bathroom Remodeling

Spa-inspired bathrooms built with premium materials.

Service Overview

Your bathroom should be a retreat. At GRIT Builders, we create spa-inspired bathrooms using premium tile, custom vanities, and quality fixtures that stand the test of time. Whether you're updating a guest bath or building your dream master suite, we manage every detail from waterproofing to final trim.

What's Included

  • Custom tile showers
  • Soaking tubs
  • Custom vanities
  • Heated flooring
  • Frameless glass enclosures
  • Premium fixtures
  • Lighting design
  • Ventilation
  • Waterproofing
  • Painting & trim

Popular Features

Walk-in Showers

Barrier-free showers with custom tile, built-in niches, and frameless glass for a modern, open feel.

Heated Floors

Radiant floor heating that keeps your bathroom warm and comfortable year-round.

Double Vanities

Custom-built double vanities with ample storage, premium countertops, and designer hardware.

Freestanding Tubs

Statement soaking tubs that create a luxurious focal point in your master bathroom.
