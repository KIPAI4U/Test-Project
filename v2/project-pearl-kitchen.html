Project Story

Client Goals

This Lake Oswego family wanted to transform their dated 1990s kitchen into a modern, open space perfect for entertaining. They needed better flow between the kitchen and living areas, more counter space, and updated finishes throughout.

Design Approach

We removed a load-bearing wall (with proper structural engineering) to create an open-concept layout. Custom shaker-style cabinetry in a soft white finish was paired with warm quartz countertops and a dramatic waterfall island.

Construction

The project was completed in 8 weeks with daily updates to the homeowners. Our crew managed plumbing relocation, electrical upgrades, custom cabinet installation, and premium tile work — all while keeping the rest of the home livable.

The Result

A bright, modern kitchen that has become the family's favorite gathering spot. The open layout connects seamlessly to the living room, and the oversized island provides both prep space and casual seating for six.
