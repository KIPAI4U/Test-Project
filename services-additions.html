Our Services

Home Additions

Expand your living space with seamless additions that look and feel like they were always part of your home.

More Space Without Moving

You love your neighborhood and your home — you just need more room. GRIT Builders designs and constructs home additions that expand your living space while maintaining the architectural integrity of your existing home.

Whether you need an extra bedroom, a larger living area, or a complete second story, we manage every detail from permits and structural engineering to finish carpentry and paint.

What's Included

  • Room additions (bedrooms, family rooms, offices)
  • Second story additions
  • Bump-out additions for kitchens or bathrooms
  • In-law suites and ADUs
  • Sunrooms and four-season rooms
  • Structural engineering and foundation work
  • Permit management and code compliance
  • Exterior matching (siding, roofing, trim)
  • HVAC extension and electrical integration
  • Interior finishing (drywall, trim, paint, flooring)

Types of Home Additions

Second Story Additions

Double your living space by adding a full or partial second floor while keeping your existing footprint.

Room Additions

Extend your home outward with new bedrooms, family rooms, or home offices that flow naturally.

In-Law Suites

Self-contained living spaces with bedroom, bathroom, and kitchenette for family or rental income.

Kitchen Bump-Outs

Expand your kitchen by extending it outward for more counter space, an island, or a dining area.
