Our Services

Whole-Home Remodeling

Complete home transformations that reimagine your entire living space from top to bottom.

Transform Your Entire Home

Sometimes a single-room remodel isn't enough. When your entire home needs updating — or when you want a cohesive, unified design throughout — a whole-home remodel is the answer. GRIT Builders specializes in comprehensive renovations that transform every room while you stay in your neighborhood.

We coordinate all trades, manage the project timeline, and ensure every space works together as a unified whole. The result is a home that feels completely new while retaining the character and location you love.

What's Included

  • Complete kitchen and bathroom remodeling
  • Living space reconfiguration and open-concept layouts
  • Flooring throughout (hardwood, tile, LVP)
  • Interior and exterior painting
  • Electrical system updates
  • Plumbing upgrades
  • HVAC improvements
  • Window and door replacement
  • Built-in storage and millwork
  • Comprehensive project management

Whole-Home Remodel Approaches

Phased Remodeling

Tackle your home in stages so you can live in parts of the house during construction. We plan the sequence for minimal disruption.

Full Gut Renovation

Strip to the studs and start fresh with new systems, layouts, and finishes for a truly like-new home.

Period Home Restoration

Preserve and restore the character of Portland's craftsman, colonial, and mid-century homes while modernizing systems and functionality.

Modern Updates

Open up floor plans, add natural light, and bring contemporary design to homes of any era.
