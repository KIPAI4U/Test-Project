Our Story

From Craft to Company

GRIT Builders began in 2001 as a small finish carpentry crew with a simple mission: do exceptional work, communicate honestly, and treat every home like our own. Our founder, James Davis, built the company one satisfied customer at a time.

Over 25 years, that small crew has grown into a full-service design-build remodeling firm serving the Portland metro area. Today, our team of designers, project managers, and skilled tradespeople works together to deliver remodels that Portland homeowners are proud of.

Our name says it all — GRIT. It's the determination to get every detail right, the perseverance to solve complex challenges, and the commitment to deliver nothing less than excellence on every project.
