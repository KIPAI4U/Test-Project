Bathroom Remodeling

Create a spa-inspired retreat right in your own home with expert bathroom design and construction.

Bathroom Remodels That Elevate Your Home

A well-designed bathroom adds comfort, value, and daily enjoyment to your home. Whether you're updating a guest bath or creating a luxurious master suite, GRIT Builders brings the design expertise and construction quality that Portland homeowners trust.

We specialize in creating bathrooms that balance beauty with practicality — spaces that look stunning and function perfectly for years to come.

What's Included

  • Walk-in shower design and installation
  • Freestanding and built-in soaking tubs
  • Custom vanity design and installation
  • Tile work (floor, wall, shower, accent)
  • Heated flooring systems
  • Lighting design and installation
  • Plumbing fixture selection and installation
  • Ventilation and moisture management
  • Storage solutions (niches, built-ins, medicine cabinets)
  • Waterproofing and code compliance

Popular Bathroom Remodel Features

Walk-In Showers

Frameless glass enclosures, rain showerheads, body jets, and custom tile work for a spa-like experience.

Double Vanities

His-and-hers vanities with custom cabinetry, stone countertops, and vessel or undermount sinks.

Heated Floors

Radiant floor heating under tile for warm, comfortable mornings year-round.

Freestanding Tubs

Statement soaking tubs that serve as the focal point of a luxurious master bathroom.
