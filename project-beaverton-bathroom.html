Beaverton Master Bathroom

Bathroom Remodel Beaverton

Project Story

The Homeowner's Challenge

This Beaverton homeowner was ready to retire a master bathroom that hadn't been updated since the home was built. The space showed its age everywhere — a green laminate countertop, worn cabinetry with doors that no longer closed properly, a cramped shower stall behind a frosted glass door, and linoleum tile flooring with a dated diamond pattern. Beyond the cosmetics, the shower was too small for comfortable daily use, the vanity offered limited storage, and the overall feel was dark and closed-in. The homeowners wanted something modern, open, and spa-like.

The Remodeling Solution

GRIT Builders delivered this project under the Signature Bath tier — a full remodel covering demolition, waterproofing, custom tile work, and fixture upgrades. Matt worked with the homeowners to maximize the existing footprint while completely transforming the space. The old vanity gave way to custom white shaker cabinetry with a polished black granite countertop and undermount square sink. A built-in medicine cabinet was recessed into the wall, flanked by recessed LED downlights for even, shadow-free lighting.

The shower saw the most dramatic upgrade — opened up into a generous walk-in with a frameless glass enclosure, floor-to-ceiling subway tile in flat and dimensional wavy patterns, and a stainless steel shower tower panel with rain head, body jets, and handheld spray. Black hexagonal tile carries from the bathroom floor into the shower, with a teal accent wall adding a bold pop of color against the white tile and black granite.

The Final Result

Where there was once a dark, dated room with worn finishes, there is now a bright, modern master bathroom that feels like a spa retreat. The white shaker cabinetry against polished black granite gives the vanity a crisp, high-end look, while the subway tile shower with its stainless steel tower panel is the centerpiece of the room. Black hexagonal floor tile ties everything together, flowing seamlessly into the shower, and the teal accent wall brings warmth and personality without overwhelming the clean material palette.
