Project Story

Client Goal

The homeowners of this West Linn residence wanted to transform their outdated master bathroom into a spa-inspired retreat. The existing bathroom had a builder-grade vanity, a dated shower-tub combo, and worn vinyl flooring that no longer matched the quality of the rest of their home.

Design Challenges

The master bathroom in this West Linn home had limited square footage, requiring creative space planning to fit both a walk-in shower and a freestanding soaking tub. The existing plumbing needed to be completely reconfigured, and we identified water damage behind the old tub surround that required remediation before new construction could begin.

Construction Process

After demolition revealed the water damage, our team repaired the subfloor and replaced damaged framing. We reconfigured the plumbing to accommodate the new layout, installed a curbless walk-in shower with a linear drain, and positioned the freestanding tub beneath the window for natural light. A custom double vanity was built with floating design for a clean, modern look.

Materials Used

The shower features large-format porcelain tile in a warm gray tone with a decorative mosaic niche. The flooring is heated porcelain tile throughout. Countertops are Carrara marble on the custom floating vanity. All fixtures are matte black from Delta, creating a striking contrast with the white and gray palette. The freestanding tub is an acrylic soaking model from Kohler.

Final Result

This bathroom remodel in West Linn transformed a dated, cramped space into a luxurious spa-like retreat. The curbless shower and freestanding tub create a sense of openness despite the room's footprint. The heated floors and rainfall showerhead add daily luxury, while the floating vanity keeps the space feeling clean and modern. The homeowners describe it as their favorite room in the house.
