Our Services

Kitchen Remodeling

Transform your kitchen into the heart of your home with expert design and craftsmanship.

Portland's Trusted Kitchen Remodeling Experts

Your kitchen is where meals are prepared, families gather, and memories are made. Whether you're updating an outdated kitchen or doing a complete transformation, GRIT Builders designs and builds kitchens that are beautiful, functional, and built to last.

From layout redesign to custom cabinetry, we handle every aspect of your kitchen remodel with the attention to detail that Portland homeowners expect.

What's Included

  • Custom cabinetry design and installation
  • Countertop selection and fabrication (quartz, granite, marble, butcher block)
  • Tile backsplash design and installation
  • Lighting design (recessed, pendant, under-cabinet)
  • Layout redesign and space optimization
  • Appliance selection and installation
  • Plumbing fixture upgrades
  • Electrical updates and code compliance
  • Flooring installation
  • Project management from start to finish

Popular Kitchen Remodel Features

Kitchen Islands

Custom islands with seating, storage, and prep space that become the centerpiece of your kitchen.

Open-Concept Layouts

Remove walls and open your kitchen to the living and dining areas for a modern, connected feel.

Walk-In Pantries

Organized storage solutions that keep your kitchen clean and clutter-free.

Custom Cabinetry

Built-to-order cabinets in your choice of wood, finish, and hardware for a one-of-a-kind look.
