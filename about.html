Meet Matt Beale

The person behind GRIT Builders — and why your home is in good hands.

Matt and Buddy — the unofficial company mascot

Founder & Owner

A Different Kind of Contractor

Matt Beale didn't take the typical path to remodeling. He grew up in a small town in Wisconsin, enlisted in the Air Force after high school, and then did something most people talk about but few actually pull off — he put himself through both an undergraduate degree and a master's program at night while working full-time and raising a family.

For 20 years, Matt built a career in operations for technology and manufacturing companies across the Portland area. He was good at it. But the work that kept calling him back — the projects he'd lose weekends to, the problems he actually wanted to solve — always involved building and transforming spaces.

In 2020, Matt made the leap. He left the corporate world to pursue what had always been his real passion: home remodeling and design. GRIT Builders was born — not from a business plan, but from a deeply held belief that homeowners deserve a contractor who cares as much about their home as they do.

— Matt

What drives Matt is simple: your project matters to him.

He brings the discipline of his military service, the operational precision from two decades in business, and a genuine love for the craft to every kitchen, bathroom, and whole-home remodel. He's not here to upsell you or rush through a job. He's here to listen to what you want, get the details right, and deliver honest, excellent work.

What Matt finds most rewarding is the moment a project comes together — when a homeowner walks into their finished kitchen or bathroom and sees their own ideas, the things they'd been imagining, built and real and better than they pictured. That's what gets him up in the morning.
